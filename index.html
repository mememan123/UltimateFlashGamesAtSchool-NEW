Ultimate Flash Games at School is being retired.

 

Hey, it's me, Ruby Kenny, the creator of Ultimate Flash Games at School and its many remade versions.

First off I would just like to say Thank You to everyone who stuck around this long, I never expected my little website that I created on a rainy day in 6th grade

to become this popular. And I hope some of you have kept around with all my updates and site moves over the years.

 

I'm now a Junior in High School, and while making this website was fun, it just isn't as easy to maintain as before. Ruffle, while amazing, isn't compatible with

most of the classic flash games from the old sites, and as a result the library of games has gone down exponentially. GitHub Pages is slow and annoying to use,

and Neocities requires payment just so i can F**king upload an SWF. Google Sites was really the best option but sadly, a lot of Firewalls for schools have

blocked any google sites made with "Game" or "Unblocked" in the title, which is why I moved to GeoCities and then GitHub, but as i already said they

have their problems which didn't motivate me to update often, and also gave me stress because I felt I was letting my audience down by updating infrequently.

I also just don't have much free time anymore, I'm busy doing other things or hanging out with friends, and the website kinda just fell to the wayside.

 

And unfortunately, with the way the internet is changing, Unblocked Games sites are dying. and i don't want to be stuck in 2016 still

making a site for old flash games when better options exist.

 

If you want to find me elsewhere, i'm on YouTube and Twitter. my discord is SupersonicRainboom#6537

 

I might make a new site on Neocities, but until then, thanks for playing.
